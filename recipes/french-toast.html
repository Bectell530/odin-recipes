French Toast

French Toast is a dish made of sliced bread soaked in beaten eggs and typically milk, then pan fried. Alternative names and variants include "eggy bread", "Bombay toast", "gypsy toast" and "poor knights" (of Windsor).

It's served as a sweet dish where milk, sugar, vanilla or cinnamon are communly added before pan-frying. Then it may be topped with powdered sugar, butter, fruit, or syrup.

Cooking Time:

Prep: 10mins

Cook: 20mins

Total: 30mins

Servings: 12

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 12 thick slices of bread

Instructions:

  1. Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in the milk. Whisk in the salt, eggs, cinnamon, vanilla extract and sugar until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oil griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Soak bread slices in the mixture until saturated. Cook bread on each side until golden brown. Serve hot.
