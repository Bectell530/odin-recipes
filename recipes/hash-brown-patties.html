Hash Brown Patties

...

Cooking Time:

Prep: 15mins

Cook: 45mins

Total: 60mins

Servings: 9

Ingredients:

  • 4 red potatoes
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 eggs, whisked
  • ¼ cup fresh chives, chopped
  • 1 tsp garlic salt
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp pepper

Instructions:

  1. Peel and grate the potatoes.
  2. Drop shredded potatoes into a large bowl of ice water to remove surface starch.
  3. Preheat oven to 400°F (200˚C).
  4. Strain or squeeze potato shreds and transfer to a large bowl.
  5. Add butter, cheese, eggs, chives, garlic salt, oregano, and pepper in with the shredded potatoes. Mix together.
  6. Take a handful of the mixture and pack it on a baking sheet into desired hash brown shape.
  7. Bake for 40-45 minutes or until brown and crispy.
  8. Allow to cool for 5 minutes. Serve warm.
